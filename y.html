About Us

"Where is my Bus?" is an innovative bus tracking and booking platform designed to simplify your daily commute. Our user-friendly website provides real-time bus location updates, accurate arrival predictions, and seamless online booking capabilities. Whether you're a daily commuter or an occasional traveler, our system helps you plan your journey efficiently with live tracking, route information, and schedule alerts. The intuitive interface displays buses moving along realistic routes, giving you a clear visual representation of their progress. With additional features like contact information and an about section, we aim to create a transparent and reliable public transportation experience. Save time, reduce wait periods, and travel smarter with our comprehensive bus tracking solution that puts control of your commute right at your fingertips.
